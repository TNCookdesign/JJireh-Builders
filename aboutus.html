About Us

J Jireh Builders Inc. has worked on some of the most well-known developments in Southern California.

Founded in 1997, our experienced team of estimators and builders together have almost 40 years of experience in concrete structures. Our years working in Southern California have been characterized by well-made structures and our dependable staff. Our company thrives on satisfied customers and with safety first. Our experience has given us time to develop reliable resourcing for projects. We avoid costly delays and allow your job to be done without surprises, when you need it.

Based near San Bernardino, J Jireh Builders Inc. utilizes their excellent staff to achieve small and large-scale projects on time and on budget.
