Storm Drain Structures

When it rains, what keeps our beautiful cities from flooding and our buildings safe? Storm drain structures.

J Jireh Builders Inc. specializes in creating high quality, lasting storm drain structures. Our team crafts concrete structures to ensure your homes and businesses don’t go under water.

The seemingly mundane construction of storm drain structures, is essential to our infrastructure and at J Jireh we pride ourselves in knowing that when the job is done, your project will be functioning the way they are designed to.

We continuously strive to discover new technologies and more efficient ways to construct concrete catch basins, RCBs, head walls, and channels.
